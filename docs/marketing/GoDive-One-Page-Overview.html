GoDive

A modern logbook and ocean companion for scuba divers and snorkelers.

Log your dives

Import your dive and snorkel activities or create them directly in the app. Track your stats, notes, and media in one place.

Explore sites

Explore dive sites around the world, rate the places you have visited, and plan where you want to go next with the trip planner.

Connect with buddies

Share your dive and snorkel experiences with buddies, plan trips together, and share your favorite sightings with others.

Tag marine life

Browse the species field guide and tag the critters you've spotted on a dive or snorkel.

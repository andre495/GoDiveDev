# Explore

The **Explore** tab helps you discover and manage **dive sites** — both catalog sites and sites created from your imports.

## Map vs list

Use the **leading toggle** to switch modes:

=== "Map"

    Full-screen **hybrid** map (satellite plus labels) with red pins for sites.

    - Pin **labels** appear as you zoom in; fewer labels when zoomed out.  
    - Tap a pin, then the label, to open site detail when available.  
    - **My Sites** / **All Sites** scope filter which pins appear.

=== "List"

    Logbook-style rows grouped by **country**, with dive counts when you’ve logged dives there.

    - Each row shows site name, coordinates, and place hierarchy (country, region, body of water).  
    - Tap a row for site detail.

## Find dive sites

Use the **Search** tab and tap **Sites** (or type in the search field) to filter by site name, country, region, or body of water. See [Search](search.md).

## My Sites vs All Sites

Toggle at the bottom of the screen:

- **My Sites** — sites you’ve logged dives at or added yourself  
- **All Sites** — full catalog including reference sites you haven’t visited  

Changing scope refreshes the map pins or list.

## Site detail

Tap a site to open an overview with:

- Hero **media** from dives at that site, or a **map** when no media exists  
- **Dive count** and optional rating  
- Pager sections: **Dive details**, **Dives here**, **Marine life here**, **Tagged media**  

Swipe between sections. Open any dive or species from these lists.

## Add a dive site

Tap **+** (trailing on Explore chrome) to add a site manually:

- Name and place fields  
- Pick coordinates on a map or enter them  

You can also create sites during dive import or when a dive has GPS but no matching catalog name.

## Linking dives to sites

When you import or edit a dive, GoDive tries to link it to a catalog site by **exact site name** or **GPS proximity**. Linked sites drive map pins, Explore counts, and consistent naming on dive detail.

## Trip planner connection

Saved **planned sites** on a trip appear in Trip detail and on trip maps. Explore rows opened from a trip omit personal dive counts when showing plan-only sites.

Reference sites in **All Sites** come from the bundled [OpenDiveMap](https://opendivemap.com/) catalog — see [Acknowledgments](acknowledgments.md).
